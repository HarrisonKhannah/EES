Dr Rajiv Shah completed his specialist training at the Sydney Eye Hospital and obtained his Fellowship in glaucoma at Moorfields Eye Hospital, London.

Dr Shah has a keen interest in Glaucoma and Cataract surgery and Laser Treatments for Glaucoma.

Dr Khannah graduated from the University of Sydney and underwent specialist training at Sydney Eye Hospital and its affiliated teaching hospitals.

He has also recently published a chapter in a book regarding cataract surgery techniques.

Dr Mark Gorbatov trained to be an ophthalmologist at Sydney Eye Hospital, having obtained his medical qualifications from the University of Sydney. He was the recipient of the University Medal. He further undertook fellowship training in retinal diseases at the Lions Eye Institute in Perth and Manchester Royal Eye Hospital in the United Kingdom. He is currently a member of the Retinal Unit at Sydney Eye Hospital where he trains retinal fellows, ophthalmology registrars and medical students.
As a vitreoretinal surgeon, Dr Gorbatov is well credentialed in all aspects of retinal surgery and has special interests in small gauge surgical repair of retinal detachment, vitreous haemorrhage, macular hole, epiretinal membrane, diabetic retinopathy and related disorders. His other special interest includes the treatment of age-related macular degeneration (AMD) including the new injectable medications, and many diagnostic technologies such as optical coherence tomography (OCT).
Dr Mark Gorbatov performs intravitreal injections at Eastwood Eye Surgery.

]]> BSc(Med) MB BS(Hons 1), PhD(UNSW), FRANZCO Consultant Ophthalmic Surgeon

Dr Chang’s clinical expertise in ocular inflammation and retinal diseases is complemented by his strong academic and research credentials in this field. He was awarded a PhD in ocular immunology, and is an Adjunct Senior Lecturer at the University of NSW. Dr Chang has published extensively in the peer-reviewed literature.
Dr Chang’s areas of clinical expertise include the diagnosis and treatment of age-related macular degeneration (AMD), diabetic retinopathy, retinal vascular diseases and uveitis. He is experienced in performing retinal laser treatment, intravitreal injection therapies, and performing cataract surgery in the context of uveitis or retinal disease.
